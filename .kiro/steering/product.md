# Product: Datastunt Labs Landing Page

Datastunt Labs is an AI lab that helps businesses deliver practical, revenue-driven AI outcomes. The company operates with a venture-studio mindset — fast validation (2–3 weeks), founder-led execution, and aligned incentives.

## Core Services

- **Knowledge AI Chatbot (RAG)** — decision-intelligence systems for support, internal knowledge, and compliance
- **Document AI Suite** — OCR and process automation for claims, KYC, contracts, and forms
- **AI Marketing Assist** — personalized campaigns, content generation, and lifecycle messaging
- **Computer Vision Automation** — catalog tagging, quality inspection, and visual data extraction

## Target Audience

Founders building their second or third company, operators and CXOs launching AI ventures tied to KPIs, and capital partners seeking execution over advice.

## Content Sources

Blog content (stories, insights, articles) is fetched from the WordPress REST API at `https://blog.datastunt.com`. Contact form submissions are stored in Firebase Realtime Database.

## Pages

- `/` — Home (hero carousel, features, content sections, insights, articles)
- `/about` — Company overview
- `/contact` — Contact form
- `/industries` — Industries served
- `/rag` — Knowledge AI Chatbot service page
- `/document-suite` — Document AI Suite service page
- `/marketing-assist` — AI Marketing Assist service page
- `/computer-vision` — Computer Vision Automation service page
